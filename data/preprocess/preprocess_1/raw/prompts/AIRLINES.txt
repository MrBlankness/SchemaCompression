[DATABASE SCHEMA]
Here is the full schema of the database:

Table: AIRLINES.BOARDING_PASSES
Columns: flight_id, ticket_no, seat_no, boarding_no

Table: AIRLINES.AIRPORTS_DATA
Columns: airport_code, coordinates, airport_name, timezone, city

Table: AIRLINES.TICKETS
Columns: ticket_no, passenger_id, book_ref

Table: AIRLINES.AIRCRAFTS_DATA
Columns: range, aircraft_code, model

Table: AIRLINES.FLIGHTS
Columns: aircraft_code, actual_departure, departure_airport, scheduled_departure, flight_id, scheduled_arrival, actual_arrival, arrival_airport, status, flight_no

Table: AIRLINES.BOOKINGS
Columns: book_date, total_amount, book_ref

Table: AIRLINES.SEATS
Columns: seat_no, fare_conditions, aircraft_code

Table: AIRLINES.TICKET_FLIGHTS
Columns: amount, ticket_no, flight_id, fare_conditions
