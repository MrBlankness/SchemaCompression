[STANDALONE]
## IOWA_LIQUOR_SALES.SALES
vendor_name: TEXT | The vendor name of the company for the brand of liquor ordered
item_number: TEXT | Item number for the individual liquor product ordered.
address: TEXT | Address of store who ordered the liquor.
bottle_volume_ml: NUMBER | Volume of each liquor bottle ordered in milliliters.
city: TEXT | City where the store who ordered the liquor is located
invoice_and_item_number: TEXT | Concatenated invoice and line number associated with the liquor order. This provides a unique identifier for the individual liquor products included in the store order.
state_bottle_cost: FLOAT | The amount that Alcoholic Beverages Division paid for each bottle of liquor ordered
state_bottle_retail: FLOAT | The amount the store paid for each bottle of liquor ordered
vendor_number: TEXT | The vendor number of the company for the brand of liquor ordered
pack: NUMBER | The number of bottles in a case for the liquor ordered
category_name: TEXT | Category of the liquor ordered.
store_name: TEXT | Name of store who ordered the liquor.
item_description: TEXT | Description of the individual liquor product ordered.
sale_dollars: FLOAT | Total cost of liquor order (number of bottles multiplied by the state bottle retail)
store_location: BINARY | Location of store who ordered the liquor. The Address, City, State and Zip Code are geocoded to provide geographic coordinates. Accuracy of geocoding is dependent on how well the address is interpreted and the completeness of the reference data used.
zip_code: TEXT | Zip code where the store who ordered the liquor is located
county_number: TEXT | Iowa county number for the county where store who ordered the liquor is located
county: TEXT | County where the store who ordered the liquor is located
volume_sold_liters: FLOAT | Total volume of liquor ordered in liters. (i.e. (Bottle Volume (ml) x Bottles Sold)/1,000)"
store_number: TEXT | Unique number assigned to the store who ordered the liquor.
bottles_sold: NUMBER | The number of bottles of liquor ordered by the store
category: TEXT | Category code associated with the liquor ordered
date: DATE | Date of order
volume_sold_gallons: FLOAT | Total volume of liquor ordered in gallons. (i.e. (Bottle Volume (ml) x Bottles Sold)/3785.411784)"
